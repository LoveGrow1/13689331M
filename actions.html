Action recommendations

Practice Active Listening:

When she's sharing her feelings or concerns, make sure to show empathy and give her your full attention. This not only includes listening to her words but also understanding her emotions.

Reinforce Positive Communication:

Reiterate to her that she can freely express her wants and needs without fearing your reaction. Make an effort to respond to her concerns with understanding and kindness, even when you might not completely agree.

Celebrate Your Long-lasting Love

As you've been together for 96 months, take a moment to look back and celebrate this journey together. Organize a special day or trip to appreciate your past, revel in your present, and look forward to your future together. This can also provide a platform to discuss your physical intimacy in a safe and romantic context.
